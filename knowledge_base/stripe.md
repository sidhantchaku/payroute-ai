# Stripe Payment Gateway

## Overview
Stripe is the world's leading payment infrastructure platform, founded in 2010. Known for its developer-first approach and extensive global coverage. Ideal for international businesses and SaaS companies.

## Supported Countries
- Available in 46+ countries as a merchant
- Accepts payments from 195+ countries
- Supports 135+ currencies
- Best suited for: Global/international businesses

## Supported Payment Methods
- Credit & Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, UnionPay)
- Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay
- SEPA Direct Debit (Europe)
- iDEAL (Netherlands)
- SOFORT (Germany, Austria)
- Giropay (Germany)
- Bancontact (Belgium)
- ACH Direct Debit (USA)
- BECS Direct Debit (Australia)
- Klarna, Afterpay (BNPL)
- Link (Stripe's 1-click checkout)

## Transaction Fees
- Standard Cards (domestic): 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- International Cards: 3.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Currency conversion: +1.5%
- ACH Direct Debit: 0.8% (capped at $5)
- Radar (fraud protection): $0.05 per screened transaction (free on standard)
- Custom enterprise pricing available

## Success Rates
- Domestic cards (US/EU): 93-97%
- International cards: 88-94%
- Digital wallets: 95-98%
- Bank debits: 88-93%
- Adaptive Acceptance (ML retry): increases rates by ~3-5%

## Settlement Timeline
- Standard: 2 business days (T+2)
- Instant Payouts: within 30 minutes (1.5% fee, min $0.50)
- International: 3-7 business days
- New accounts: 7-day rolling reserve initially

## Transaction Limits
- No hard limits per transaction (varies by risk assessment)
- High-volume merchants can process millions per day
- Dispute protection available via Stripe Radar

## Merchant Categories Supported
- SaaS / Software (subscription-heavy)
- E-commerce (global)
- Marketplaces (Stripe Connect)
- On-demand platforms (Uber-style)
- Fintech / Financial Services
- Healthcare (HIPAA compliant configurations)
- EdTech (global)
- Non-profits (discounted rates)
- Crypto (some restrictions)

## Compliance & Certifications
- PCI DSS Level 1
- SOC 1 and SOC 2 (Type II)
- ISO 27001 certified
- GDPR compliant
- PSD2 / SCA compliant (Europe)
- HIPAA eligible

## Strengths
- World-class developer experience
- Stripe Connect for complex marketplace flows
- Stripe Billing for subscription management
- Stripe Radar (ML-based fraud detection)
- Stripe Sigma for analytics (SQL queries on payment data)
- Stripe Terminal for in-person payments
- Global payment method support

## Weaknesses
- Not directly available as a merchant in India (requires Stripe Atlas or Indian entity abroad)
- Higher base fees for low-ticket transactions
- Complex pricing for multi-currency international operations
- Dispute fees: $15 per chargeback

## Best For
- Global SaaS companies
- International e-commerce
- Marketplace platforms
- Subscription billing businesses
- Companies needing global payment method coverage
