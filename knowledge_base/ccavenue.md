# CCAvenue Payment Gateway

## Overview
CCAvenue is India's pioneer payment gateway, launched in 2001 by Infibeam Avenues. It is one of the most widely used gateways among traditional Indian businesses, banks, and government entities. Known for widest payment option coverage in India.

## Supported Countries
- India (primary)
- 27 major international currencies supported
- Accept cards from 200+ countries
- Best suited for: Traditional Indian businesses, government, and education institutions

## Supported Payment Methods
- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Diners, JCB, Rupay)
- Debit Cards (all major Indian bank debit cards, 300+ banks)
- Net Banking (200+ banks — widest coverage in India)
- UPI
- Wallets (all major wallets)
- EMI (50+ banks and NBFCs)
- Multi-currency international payments
- Prepaid cards
- Mobile payments

## Transaction Fees
- Domestic Cards: 2.0-2.5%
- UPI: 0% (zero MDR)
- Net Banking: 1.75-2.25%
- International Cards: 3.0-4.5%
- EMI: 2.0-3.5%
- Wallets: 1.5-2.0%
- Annual maintenance charge: INR 1,200/year (basic plan)

## Success Rates
- Domestic Cards: 85-91%
- Net Banking: 82-88% (highest coverage but variable success)
- UPI: 90-94%
- International Cards: 79-86%
- Wallets: 88-92%

## Settlement Timeline
- Standard: T+3 to T+5 (slower than competitors)
- Express: T+2 (additional fee)
- International: T+7 to T+15
- No instant settlement option

## Transaction Limits
- As per card/bank limits
- High-value transactions supported with prior approval
- No per-transaction cap for most merchants

## Merchant Categories Supported
- Government portals and PSUs
- Educational institutions (schools, colleges, universities)
- E-commerce (traditional retail)
- Hospitality (hotels, resorts)
- Healthcare
- Utilities
- Religious trusts and NGOs
- Travel agencies

## Compliance & Certifications
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified
- RBI licensed
- NPCI certified
- Listed on NSE/BSE (Infibeam Avenues)
- 3D Secure compliant

## Strengths
- Widest net banking coverage (200+ banks) in India
- Long-standing trust with banks and government
- Good for traditional industries resistant to new gateways
- CCAvenue Locker for saved card data
- Multi-currency checkout
- Detailed MIS reporting

## Weaknesses
- Outdated API and developer experience
- Slower settlement times
- Less innovation compared to Razorpay/Cashfree
- Customer support can be slow
- No modern payout infrastructure
- Not ideal for startups or tech-first companies

## Best For
- Government and PSU payment portals
- Educational institution fee collection
- Traditional Indian businesses (SMBs, family businesses)
- High net banking transaction volume
- Businesses needing widest bank coverage
