# PayU Payment Gateway

## Overview
PayU is one of India's most established payment gateways, operating since 2011. Part of the Prosus group, it has strong presence in India, Eastern Europe, Latin America, and Africa. Excellent for mid-to-large enterprises.

## Supported Countries
- India (primary, INR)
- Poland, Czech Republic, Hungary (Eastern Europe)
- Brazil, Colombia, Mexico (Latin America)
- South Africa, Nigeria (Africa)
- 50+ countries internationally
- Best suited for: Multi-regional businesses with India presence

## Supported Payment Methods
- UPI (all major apps)
- Credit & Debit Cards (all major networks)
- Net Banking (60+ banks)
- Wallets (Paytm, Amazon Pay, Mobikwik, Freecharge)
- EMI (Standard and No-Cost EMI)
- BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later via LazyPay)
- PayU Token Hub (card tokenization)
- QR Code payments

## Transaction Fees
- Domestic Cards: 1.99-2.5% per transaction
- UPI: 0% (zero MDR)
- Net Banking: 1.5-2.0%
- International Cards: 3-4.5% depending on currency
- EMI: 2-3% depending on bank and tenure
- Wallets: 1.75-2.5%
- Volume discounts available for INR 10L+ monthly volume

## Success Rates
- UPI: 92-96%
- Domestic Cards: 87-92%
- Net Banking: 84-89%
- International Cards: 80-87%
- EMI: 88-93%

## Settlement Timeline
- Standard: T+2 business days
- Express Settlements: T+1 (additional fee applies)
- International: T+5 to T+10
- PayU Instant Settlement available for eligible merchants

## Transaction Limits
- UPI: INR 1,00,000 per transaction
- Cards: INR 5,00,000 per transaction
- Net Banking: Bank-specific limits
- Daily limits: INR 10,00,000 default, customizable

## Merchant Categories Supported
- E-commerce (fashion, electronics, FMCG)
- Travel (flights, hotels, bus booking)
- Utilities (electricity, water, recharge)
- Education (school fees, edtech)
- Insurance premium collection
- Government payments (some categories)
- Healthcare
- Real estate bookings

## Compliance & Certifications
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified
- RBI licensed Payment Aggregator
- ISO 27001 certified
- 3D Secure 2.0 supported
- NPCI certified for UPI
- Tokenization compliant (CoF tokenization)

## Strengths
- Strong enterprise customer support (dedicated account managers)
- PayU Checkout (pre-built checkout with high conversion)
- Strong EMI and BNPL product
- Multi-currency support for international expansions
- PayU Risk Engine (fraud prevention)
- Excellent utilities and bill payment infrastructure

## Weaknesses
- Developer documentation less refined vs Razorpay/Stripe
- API integration can be complex
- International support less comprehensive than Stripe
- Smaller engineering community/ecosystem

## Best For
- Large enterprises and established businesses
- Utility and bill payment platforms
- Travel booking platforms
- Multi-regional businesses (India + LatAm + Eastern Europe)
- High-volume EMI transactions
