# Razorpay Payment Gateway

## Overview
Razorpay is India's leading payment gateway, founded in 2014. It is ideal for Indian businesses accepting domestic payments and supports a wide range of payment methods popular in India.

## Supported Countries
- Primary: India (INR)
- International: 100+ countries via international cards
- Best suited for: India-first businesses

## Supported Payment Methods
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Rupay)
- Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay)
- Net Banking (50+ banks)
- Wallets (Paytm, Mobikwik, Freecharge, Amazon Pay)
- EMI (No-cost EMI on major cards)
- Pay Later (Simpl, LazyPay, HDFC PayZapp)
- Bank Transfer / NEFT

## Transaction Fees
- Domestic Cards: 2% per transaction
- UPI: 0% (zero MDR for UPI transactions)
- Net Banking: 1.5-2% per transaction
- International Cards: 3% per transaction
- EMI: 1.5-3% depending on tenure
- Wallets: 1.75-2% per transaction
- Minimum fee: INR 0 per transaction
- No setup fee for standard plan

## Success Rates
- UPI transactions: 94-97%
- Domestic cards: 88-93%
- Net banking: 85-91%
- International cards: 82-88%
- Wallets: 90-94%

## Settlement Timeline
- T+2 days for most payment methods
- Instant settlements available (extra 0.25% fee)
- UPI settlements: T+1
- International card settlements: T+3 to T+7

## Transaction Limits
- UPI: Max INR 1,00,000 per transaction
- Cards: Up to INR 10,00,000 per transaction
- Net Banking: Depends on bank limits
- Wallets: INR 10,000 to INR 1,00,000 based on KYC

## Merchant Categories Supported
- E-commerce (all categories)
- SaaS / Software subscriptions
- EdTech (education platforms)
- HealthTech (hospitals, clinics, pharmacies)
- Travel & Hospitality
- Gaming (skill-based games only)
- BFSI (insurance, mutual funds)
- NGOs & Nonprofits
- Food Delivery

## Compliance & Certifications
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified
- RBI regulated Payment Aggregator
- 3D Secure 2.0 (3DS2) supported
- SEBI registered for mutual fund payments

## Strengths
- Best-in-class UPI support
- Strong developer APIs and documentation
- Razorpay Route for marketplace payouts
- Subscription and recurring billing support
- Excellent dashboard analytics
- Fraud detection with Razorpay Shield

## Weaknesses
- Limited international coverage compared to Stripe
- Not ideal for pure cross-border payment businesses
- Occasional UPI downtime during peak festival seasons

## Best For
- Indian startups and SMBs
- High UPI transaction volume businesses
- Marketplace platforms needing payouts
- Subscription-based businesses in India
