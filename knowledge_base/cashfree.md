# Cashfree Payments Gateway

## Overview
Cashfree Payments is India's fastest growing payment gateway, founded in 2015. Known for fastest settlement cycles in India and strong payout infrastructure. Backed by Apis Partners and Y Combinator.

## Supported Countries
- India (primary)
- Cross-border payouts to 150+ countries
- Accept international cards from 100+ countries
- Best suited for: Indian businesses needing fast settlements and payout capabilities

## Supported Payment Methods
- UPI (all apps including UPI Autopay for subscriptions)
- Credit & Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Rupay)
- Net Banking (65+ banks)
- Wallets (all major wallets)
- EMI (No-cost and standard)
- BNPL (ZestMoney, Simpl, LazyPay)
- App-based payments
- QR Codes (static and dynamic)

## Transaction Fees
- Domestic Cards: 1.75-2.0% per transaction
- UPI: 0% (zero MDR)
- Net Banking: 1.5-1.75%
- International Cards: 2.95-3.5%
- EMI: 1.5-2.5%
- Wallets: 1.75-2.0%
- One of the most competitive pricing in India

## Success Rates
- UPI: 95-98% (industry-leading)
- Domestic Cards: 89-94%
- Net Banking: 86-91%
- International Cards: 83-88%
- Overall platform uptime: 99.95%

## Settlement Timeline
- Instant Settlements: Real-time (within 15 minutes)
- Same Day Settlement: Available for eligible merchants
- Standard: T+2
- International payouts: 1-3 business days
- Cashfree offers fastest settlement in India

## Transaction Limits
- UPI: INR 1,00,000 per transaction
- Cards: INR 10,00,000 per transaction
- Payouts: INR 10,00,000 per transaction
- Bulk payouts: No hard limit for enterprise

## Compliance & Certifications
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified
- RBI licensed Payment Aggregator (PA)
- NPCI certified
- ISO 27001 certified
- SOC 2 Type II
- SEBI registered for MF payments
- 3DS 2.0 compliant

## Strengths
- Fastest settlement in India (instant and same-day)
- Cashfree Payouts: Best-in-class bulk disbursement platform
- UPI AutoPay for recurring payments
- Superb API documentation and SDKs
- Strong gaming and lending sector support
- AutoCollect: Virtual accounts for B2B reconciliation
- Lowest MDR rates in the market

## Weaknesses
- Smaller brand recognition vs Razorpay for consumer-facing businesses
- International acceptance (incoming) less mature than Stripe
- Fewer BNPL partners compared to PayU
- Limited point-of-sale / offline features

## Best For
- Businesses needing fastest settlement
- Lending and NBFC platforms
- Gig economy and marketplace payouts
- Real-money gaming platforms
- High-volume UPI transaction businesses
- Businesses with bulk disbursement needs
